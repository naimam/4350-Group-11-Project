FOOD HUNT

A website that allows users to find recipes and restaurants for foods they are craving.

Learn More... or Sign Up!



About

Problem: Sometimes we have ingredients, but have no idea what to make with them. Other times, we may not want to cook, but still want to eat food with those ingredients. Furthermore, there may be some days where you just have no clue what meals to make. We wanted an app that lets us solve all of these problems.

Solution: We aim to deliver an app that not only allows users to find recipes and restaurants for foods they are craving, but also allows users to create meal plans based on their dietary & health prefrences. The app will allow users to create accounts so they can save recipes and store their location to view restaurants in their area. Users will be able to search for recipes based on various ingredients/keywords. If the user cannot find a suitable recipe, they will also be able to search for restaurants to satisfy their cravings.

Features

Recipe Search

Do you want to make Chicken Alfredo but have no idea how to do so? Use the Recipe Search feature to get a list of recipes, with each recipe including information on the calories, cooking time, health/diet labels, ingredients, and a link to the steps!

Restaurant Search

Maybe you want to get some Pizza in your area... You can use the Restaurant Search feature to search for restaurants by inputing your zip code and whichever cuisine you want. For each restaurant, there is information on their address, phone number, rating, price, and a link to their yelp page.

Meal Planner

What if you have no idea what to eat for the day- or even for the week? The Meal Planner feature is perfect for you! By filling out a survey, the meal planner displays meals tailored to your dietary & health needs!

Account Creation

By creating an account, you are able to save and view your favorite recipes and restaurants. Plus, you can even store your meal planners!
